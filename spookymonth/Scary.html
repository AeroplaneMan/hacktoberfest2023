October has one date people are mostly focused on and that is...

HALLOWEEN!!!!!!!!!

So here, I'll tell you the good halloween candy to give/get.

Snickers

Snickers are a great candy to give away and get for trick or treating, the small sizes are ideal since the regular sized bars are probably too expensive.

Milky Way

Milky Ways are also a great candy. It's basically like Snickers with caramel inside. Again the smaller ones are more ideal.

Twix

Are you left Twix or right Twix? Either way it's a good candy to get, it's like a Milky Way but more crunchier.

I don't have time to do other candies. but I hoped you liked my small list of some Halloween candy.
